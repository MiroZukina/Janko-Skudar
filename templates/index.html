My Journey to Psychotherapy

Hi, I’m Janko Skudar. My path to becoming a therapist was not a straight line, but it has given me a unique perspective on change and personal growth. After graduating with a degree in petroleum engineering and spending years traveling and exploring different careers, I realized my true calling was in helping others navigate their inner worlds.

This realization led me to Ireland, where I dedicated myself to a 4-year journey to become a psychotherapist. I've since worked with diverse clients at organizations like Pieta House and within the Irish prison system, focusing on depression, trauma, and addiction. I am passionate about this work and feel privileged to support people on their path to healing.

My Therapeutic Approach

Therapy is a collaborative journey, and my role is to provide a safe, confidential, and non-judgemental space for your story.

Integrative Therapy

I don't believe in a "one-size-fits-all" approach. I draw from various modalities (like CBT, Humanistic, and Psychodynamic) to tailor therapy specifically to your unique needs and goals.

Your First Session

The first session is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. We will discuss what brought you to therapy, your hopes, and any concerns you may have. It's a gentle, pressure-free start.

A Safe Space

My commitment is to create an environment of trust and empathy. Here, you can explore difficult thoughts and feelings, knowing you will be met with acceptance and professional support.
